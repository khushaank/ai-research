Your Learning Path

As a CA student who already knows the basics, your next step is to master functions and tools that handle large datasets, automate tasks, and perform complex financial analysis. Start by deeply understanding lookup functions like VLOOKUP and the more powerful XLOOKUP. Then, move to data aggregation with PivotTables, which are fundamental in auditing and reporting. After that, explore Power Query to automate data cleaning and transformation. Finally, delve into financial modeling and dashboard creation to learn how to present your findings effectively. Use the 'Top Channels' and 'Curated Courses' tabs to find resources for each of these areas.
