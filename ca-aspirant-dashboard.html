Your 6-Month Action Plan

Follow this structured path for steady progress. Select a timeframe to see your goals.

Phase 1: Foundational Focus

  • Communication: Join a Toastmasters club. Goal: Attend 4 meetings.
  • Digital: Master Advanced Excel functions via YouTube.
  • Effectiveness: Use the Pomodoro Technique for your toughest subject.

Phase 2: Intermediate Application

  • Communication: Explain a complex topic to your study group weekly.
  • Digital: Complete a free online beginner's course in Tally Prime.
  • Effectiveness: Use Trello or Notion to map out your weekly study plan.

Phase 3: Advanced Integration

  • Communication: Write short finance news summaries on LinkedIn.
  • Digital: Explore a 'Getting Started' module for Microsoft Power BI.
  • Effectiveness: Conduct a monthly review of your goals and adjust your strategy.
