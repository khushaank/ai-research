Your Roadmap to Ikigai

A goal without a plan is just a wish. Here is a strategic roadmap for the highly ambitious role of a FinTech Innovator / Founder, outlining key milestones from learning to launch.

  1. Phase 1: Foundational Learning

    Complete certifications in Blockchain and advanced Financial Modeling. Deep-dive into regulatory compliance (RegTech).

  2. Phase 2: Practical Experience

    Build a prototype for a decentralized payment solution or a robo-advisor. Participate in a FinTech hackathon to validate ideas quickly.

  3. Phase 3: Networking & Validation

    Join a FinTech-focused startup incubator. Actively engage with VCs and angel investors to refine your business model and pitch.

  4. Goal: Secure Seed Funding

    Within 1-3 years, secure seed funding based on a validated Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and begin scaling operations.
